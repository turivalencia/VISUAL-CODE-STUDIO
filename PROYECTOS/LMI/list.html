THREE CHOCOLATES CAKE

INGREDIENTS

  • Base:
    • A packet of biscuits.
    • A tablespoon of melted butter
  • Black layer
    • 250 g of dark chocolate for desserts.
    • A packet of curd
    • 200 ml of milk
    • 200 ml of cream
  • Brown layer
    • 250 g of milk chocolate
    • 250 g of milk chocolate.
    • 200 ml of milk
    • 200 ml of cream
  • White layer:
    • 250 g of white chocolate.
    • A packet of curd
    • 200 ml of milk
    • 200 ml of cream

PREPARATION

  1. First, prepare the base: Crush the biscuits with the chopped butter. Pour it into a springform pan, spreading it well over the base, and put it in the oven for a few minutes so that it becomes firm and crunchy.

  3. Next, do the same with the milk chocolate and pour the dark chocolate on top. To prevent a hole from forming in the previous mixture, break up the waterfall by letting the liquid fall onto a spoon.
  4. And then do the same with the white chocolate
